About

Freight Class Calculator estimates NMFC density-based freight class from your shipment's dimensions and weight, cross-references both the official FCDC density table and the July 2025 13-sub density scale, and previews how the load fits into a truck or container in 3D. It supports batch mode for multi-item shipments, a reverse calculator, and palletizing with real per-carton layouts — all directly in your browser, with no installation or account required.

This site is built and maintained independently by a developer focused on free, practical logistics tools. It is not affiliated with NMFTA or any carrier. Estimates are density-based only; for binding classification, consult NMFTA ClassIT+ or your carrier. If you have questions, feedback, or found a calculation that looks wrong, see the Contact page.
